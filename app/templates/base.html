What's a Smart City?

A Smart City is a modern urban area that uses various types of electronic methods and sensors to collect data. This data is then analyzed to manage assets, resources, and services efficiently. The goal is to enhance the quality of life for citizens, promote sustainability, and create an efficient infrastructure.
